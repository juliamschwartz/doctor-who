Nicholas Briggs

Nicholas Briggs (born 29 September 1961 in London, England)[1] is a British actor and writer, predominantly associated with the BBCscience fiction television series Doctor Who and its various spin-offs, particularly as the voice of the Daleks and the Cybermen. Briggs sometimes uses the pseudonym Arthur Wallis.
